About FreshTrackPro

Revolutionizing Fresh Food Vending with Intelligent Monitoring

What is FreshTrackPro?

FreshTrackPro is an innovative fruit freshness vending machine designed to ensure the quality and safety of fresh produce in supermarkets. Using real-time sensor data, it monitors temperature, humidity, and gas levels to maintain optimal freshness.

By integrating IoT technology, our system provides real-time cloud updates, automated disposal of spoiled items, and dynamic pricing for near-expiry products.
